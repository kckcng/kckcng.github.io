Final Year Project

COMP 4981 | Summer 2021 - Spring 2022 | HKUST
Title: UniFi: Machine learning-based Investment Guidance System for University Students
Supervisor: Prof. Dik Lun Lee
Abstract: Despite the increased interest in investments, university students lack financial literacy and specialized recommendation in stock trading. In this project, we created a financial prediction and portfolio advising system, where young investors are provided with customized portfolio recommendations, price trend predictions, as well as financial information and knowledge. Rigorous experiments on price prediction were conducted and existing research was extended by enhancing price prediction models with correlations that achieved a prediction accuracy of 98.47%. Optimized portfolio recommendations were generated based on machine learning predictions and tested trading strategies, and achieved a 15% annualized return (3% higher than the market) for 6.5 years. While university students can learn to invest individually or as a group, our system will assist the young and inexperienced in making more intelligent investment decisions on their journey towards early financial freedom.
#Python #Tensorflow #Darts #ReactJS #NodeJS #MySQL
